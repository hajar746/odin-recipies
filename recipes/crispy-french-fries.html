Crispy French Fries

Description

Make the crispsiest french fries ever. Better than restaurant ones!

Ingredients Needed:

  • potatoes
  • salt
  • oil

Steps:

  1. wash and peel potaotes
  2. cut them in long rectangular strips
  3. soak in cold water for an hour
  4. drain and pat dry the potatoes using a clean kitchen towl
  5. fry in high flame until golden and crispy
  6. remove from oil and sprinkle salt, enjoy!
