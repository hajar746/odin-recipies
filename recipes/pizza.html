Cheese Pizza

Description

Yummy! The most delicious and easy pizza recipe you'll ever find on the internet. Home-maid pizza using any topping of your choice.

Ingredients needed:

  • flour
  • yeast
  • cheese
  • tomatoes
  • toppings of choice
  • oregano

Steps:

  1. prepare the dough by adding yeast to flour and kneading with warm water or milk
  2. let dough rise for 3 hours or more
  3. prepare pizza sauce by first boiling tomatoes
  4. puree the tomatoes and cook it, add salt to taste
  5. roll out dough in circle shape and make small holes
  6. add pizza sauce, grated cheese and toppings of choice
  7. preheat oven and bake for 25 minutes, enjoy!
