Sweet N Spicy Ramen

Description

A sweet and spicy ramen recipe that only takes a few minutes to make!

Ingredients

  • 2 garlic cloves
  • green onion
  • soya sauce
  • sugar
  • dates/grape molasses
  • ramen seasonings
  • chilli flakes
  • 1 cup water for cooking

Steps:

  1. chop the garlic and green onions
  2. in a bowl mix together 1 tbs of soya sauce, 1 tsp of molasses and 1/2 tsp of sugar with 2 tbs of water and keep aside
  3. turn on the flame and add oil to pan and garlic,ramen seasonings and chilli flakes (according to spice level)
  4. cook for some time and add soya sauce mixute and cook for a minute
  5. add 1 cup of water and when it boils add 1 packet of ramen
  6. after the ramen is cooked add green onions to garnish, enjoy
