Rasberry Popsicles

These 3 ingredient popsicles are so easy to make! Fresh and healthy summer snacks.

    Ingredients needed:
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen raspberrries
  • 1/4 cup of sugar
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 cups water

Steps:

  1. 1 In a medium saucepan, add the raspberries, sugar, and water. Heat over medium heat until the mixture begins to simmer.
  2. 2 Reduce the heat and simmer for 10 minutes until the raspberries have softened and the mixture has thickened.
  3. 3 Remove from heat and puree the mixture using a blender or food processor.
  4. 4 Pour the mixture into popsicle molds. Freeze for at least 6 hours or overnight.
  5. 5 To remove the popsicles, run the molds under warm water until the popsicles slide out.
  6. 6 Serve immediately and enjoy!

    7. Note: This recipe makes 4 rolls of sushi.
