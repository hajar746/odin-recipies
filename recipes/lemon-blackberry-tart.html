Lemon Blackberry Tart

Looking for a delicious dessert that's perfect for summer? Try making our lemon blackberry tart! This recipe features a buttery shortbread crust, a tangy lemon filling, and sweet blackberries on top. It's the perfect combination of sweet and tart, and is sure to impress your friends and family.

    Ingredients needed:
    For the crust
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
    • For the filling
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon lemon zest
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • /2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • For the topping
  • 1 pint fresh blackberries
  • 1/4 cup blackberry jam

Steps:

  1. 1 Preheat the oven to 350Â°F. Grease a 9-inch tart pan with a removable bottom.
  2. 2 In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, powdered sugar, and salt. Using a pastry cutter or your fingers, cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  3. 3 Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of the prepared tart pan. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until lightly golden brown. Set aside to cool.
  4. 4 In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, sugar, lemon juice, and lemon zest until well combined. Add the flour, baking powder, and salt and whisk until smooth.
  5. 5 Pour the filling into the cooled crust and bake for 20-25 minutes, or until set and lightly golden brown. Let cool completely.
  6. 6 In a small saucepan, heat the blackberry jam over low heat until melted. Brush the melted jam over the top of the cooled tart.
  7. 7 Arrange the fresh blackberries on top of the tart in a single layer. Serve immediately, or chill until ready to serve.

    8. Note: This recipe makes 8 servings.
